Sandbox Component HTML : 025 Using Pipes

date pipe
My birthday is {{birthday | date}}
date format pipe
My birthday is {{birthday | date:"dd/MM/yyyy"}}.
date format pipe
My birth date is {{birthday | date:"dd"}}.
date format pipe
My birth month is {{birthday | date:"MM"}}.
date format pipe
My birth year is {{birthday | date:"yyyy"}}.
uppercase pipe
I love {{'cake' | uppercase}}.
lowercase pipe
I HATE {{'CATS' | lowercase}}.
date format and uppercase pipe
My birthday is {{birthday | date | uppercase}}.
lowercase pipe
I HATE {{'CATS' | lowercase}}.
currency pipe
Your total is {{total * fee | currency}}.
currency pipe
Your total is {{total * fee | currency: 'GBP'}}.
currency pipe
Your total is {{total * fee | currency: 'INR'}}.
percent pipe
Our fee is {{ per | percent}}.

Note: You can create your own custom pipes as and when required, the above mentioned are ones predefined by the Angular.
